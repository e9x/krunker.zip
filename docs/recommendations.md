# Cheat Setting Recommendations

## Aimbot

Aimbot is very risky and always ends with your account being banned. This is a problem with cheating in general, as even simple AutoHotKey scripts can be detected and result in a ban. However, if you still decide to use aimbot, here are some recommendations for settings to decrease the risk of getting caught:

1. **FOV Check:** Enable the FOV check option. This feature ensures that the aimbot will only activate when the target is within your set field of view. This will prevent your aim from snapping to targets that are off-screen or far away from your crosshair, making your gameplay appear more natural and less suspicious.

2. **Smoothness:** Set the smoothness to a higher value to make the aimbot movement appear more natural and human-like. A higher smoothness value will make the aimbot's aiming slower and less snappy, which can help avoid suspicion.

3. **Field of View (FOV):** Keep the FOV small to avoid blatant snapping to targets outside of your normal view. A smaller FOV will cause the aimbot to only lock onto targets within a limited area around your crosshair, making it less obvious that you are using an aimbot.

4. **Hitbox:** Choose a hitbox that is less obvious than the head, such as the chest or stomach. This will make your shots look less suspicious, as consistently hitting headshots can quickly raise suspicion.

5. **Aimbot Key:** Assign a specific key to activate the aimbot, such as a mouse button, rather than having it always active. This will allow you more control over when the aimbot is used and help avoid accidental or obvious aim
